    What is Thriftflow?

    Thrift flow is a mobile automated irrigation management system; a low-cost mobile based monitoring solution. An automated sprinkler irrigation system using a microcontroller and a smartphone device that will help farmers automate the distribution of water via solenoid valves, monitor the soil moisture levels using the sensor, determine the water consumption using water flow sensor and store logs of system actions.

    Benefits of Thriftflow

    Many farm and home owners are under the wrong impression that using the hose to water the lawn and using standar way of irrigation is more cost efficient than installing an automated irrigation system. On the contrary, installing automated irrigation systems is an effective way to reduce your water costs, prevent uneven watering and keep your soil healthier. Additionally, installing an efficient irrigation system can also increase the value of your property and save you time.

    Thriftflow and Agriculture

    Development of a whole farm plan for the property is a good way to start preparing for automation. During the development of a whole farm plan, landholders should consider automatic irrigation in the planning process so they can incorporate some of the features required for automation from the start. This might involve design of the channels for channel automation if that is possible or it might be the use of bay outlets and other channel structures that will suit automation at a later stage..
